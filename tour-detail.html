Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

Arrive at the destination. Meet your guide and transfer to the hotel. Evening at leisure or short local exploration.

Day 2: City Sightseeing

Start with a city tour covering historical monuments, museums, and bustling markets. Optional local cuisine tasting.

Day 3: Nature & Outdoor Activities

Spend the day enjoying nature — visit parks, botanical gardens, or enjoy a light trek or outdoor adventure activity.

Day 4: Cultural Immersion

Participate in a cultural program or workshop. Explore local traditions, art forms, and enjoy a folk music or dance show.

Day 5: Leisure & Relaxation

Free day for relaxation. Visit a spa, enjoy poolside activities, or explore the town on your own pace.

Day 6: Adventure or Day Trip

Option for a short excursion to a nearby attraction or experience thrilling activities like ziplining, rafting, or a nature hike.

Day 7: Departure

Pack up and check out. Transfer to the airport or railway station for your return journey. Tour ends with beautiful memories!
